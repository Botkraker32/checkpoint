About

Olajide Olayinka Williams "JJ" Olatunji, better known as KSI, is an English YouTuber, internet personality, rapper, actor, and boxer.
He is also a member and co-founder of the British YouTube group known as the Sidemen.
In 2009, KSI registered his YouTube account under the name KSIOlajideBT.

Work

KSI has become the second-most-popular YouTube channel in the United Kingdom, and his second channel is the sixth most popular.
He is the most-subscribed-to person of color on YouTube as well.
Variety ranked KSI as the most influential celebrity among US teens in 2015.
